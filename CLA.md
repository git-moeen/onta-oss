## How to sign

Comment the following, exactly, on your first pull request:

> I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA

The check runs automatically on every pull request
(`.github/workflows/cla.yml`). Your signature — GitHub username and
timestamp — is recorded in `.github/signatures/cla.json` on the
`cla-signatures` branch. Signing once covers all Your future
contributions to this repository.
